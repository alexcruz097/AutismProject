WHAT CAUSES AUTISM / DIAGNOSIS

Autism is something people are born with. No one has a clear indication or answers to what causes it. It probably have something to do with a child's genes and and other contributing factor surrounding how the brain is developed.
A Doctor examines a child during checkup and when there is suspicion that a child is not reaching some developmental milestones, a team of experts then determine if a child has autism or not. For example 1.If a child is not as engaged a should be at a certain stage, or deficiency in socio-emotinal reciprocracy, reduced interest, emotions, failure to initiate or respond to social interactions.

3.Total lack of facial expressions, in non verbal communication, abnormalities in eye contact, or difficient in the understanding and the use of gestures.
